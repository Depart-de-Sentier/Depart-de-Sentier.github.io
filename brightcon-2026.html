Brightcon 2026: Save the Date

Save the dates for Brightcon 2026. We are excited to announce that Brightcon 2026 will take place at Aalborg University, Denmark & online this year.

Thanks to our cohost team, led by Prof. Massimo Pizzol from the Department of Sustainability and Planning, Aalborg University, Life Cycle Sustainability (LCS) research group, for making this possible.

Whether you're joining us from your desk anywhere in the world or physically in Aalborg, this will be an exciting week to exchange the latest trends and development for LCA-based open sustainability assessment.

When
September 20–25, 2026
Format
Hybrid (Online + Aalborg University, Denmark)
Event page
https://indico.d-d-s.ch/event/2/

The Week at a Glance

  • Hackathon (Sept 21–22): Intensive collaborative sprints (In-person).
  • Courses (Sept 22): Technical deep dives for practitioners (In-person).
  • The Conference (Sept 23–25): Keynotes, community sessions, and presentations (Hybrid).

Brightcon is for everyone! From coders, researchers, consultants to curious policy makers, all of us who believe in making quantitative sustainability assessment transparent and accessible.
